Elections

We hold elections at the end of every academic year, unless there are circumstances blocking us from doing that. For example, if a core leader is graduating in the Fall and their responsibilities can't be taken over by another leader, then we may hold a nomination process for that position before the position becomes unfilled.

However, for the 2017-2018 year, we will be opening up nominations for several officer positions in the coming weeks to fill gaps left by the leadership that graduated in May 2016.
